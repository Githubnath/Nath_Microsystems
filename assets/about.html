Discover Our Journey and How We Started

Nath Microsystems was founded in 2015 by Engr. Emenike Nathaniel—a passionate technologist, visionary leader, and seasoned Electrical & Electronics Engineer with a flair for full-stack software development. What began as a solo passion project soon transformed into a dynamic digital solutions firm with a clear mission: to empower businesses through innovation, design, and intelligent technology.

From the very beginning, Engr. Nathaniel saw the web not just as a space for information, but as a canvas for connection, commerce, and creativity. His early projects combined clean aesthetics with functional power, setting a high standard for what Nath Microsystems would become.

Starting with just a laptop, big dreams, and relentless curiosity, he built the foundation of a company that now serves diverse clients with custom-built websites, scalable applications, and compelling digital experiences. Under his leadership, the firm has grown from humble beginnings to a trusted name in web development, known for quality, innovation, and reliability.

Today, Nath Microsystems stands strong on the values it was built upon—technical excellence, creative thinking, and unwavering commitment to client success—still led by its founder and CEO, Engr. Emenike Nathaniel, and still guided by the vision that started it all.
